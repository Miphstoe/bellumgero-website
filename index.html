Patch Notes

Bellum Gero Patch Notes - February 24, 2026

This update delivers community-requested PvP adjustments, expands Ranger utility, improves loot and vendor economy support, and adds new progression options.

Gameplay and Balance

  • Reverted the previous restriction and re-enabled pets in PvP at community request.
  • Increased Ranger Area Track range to 1024 meters.

Loot and Economy

  • Expanded Junk Dealer buyback items to include Bacta Tanks, Medical Consoles, and Village resources.
  • Added new DNA Samples that can now be looted directly from creatures.

Vendors and Structures

  • Holocron of Destiny can now be placed in City Halls.
  • Added a new Bellum Gero Token Vendor in Cnet offering Veteran Rewards for 75 tokens each.

Bellum Gero Patch Notes - February 17, 2026

New creature content was added to stabilize resource availability, utility rewards were expanded, city decoration options were improved, and multiple quality-of-life updates were delivered for Jedi progression and world encounters.

New Content and Features

  • Longclaw Wild Vortor Lizards were added on Lok to provide Scaley Hide.
  • Wild Foreign Bantha were added to Rori and Talus; Wild Monstrous Graul were added to Rori.
  • Radar Topography Screen can now be looted in the world or purchased for 2 Holocrons of Destiny.
  • World Boss encounters now reward 3 loot items per participant instead of 1 if the player actively engages.
  • Mayors can place untamed Bio-Engineer pet deeds as city decorations for 1,000 credits per week each.
  • Jedi Knight progression now requires spending appropriate Jedi skill points and selecting a faction.
  • The Village Rewards Vendor Buff Crystal now preserves buffs on resurrection and has 5 charges.

Fixes and Improvements

  • Fixed naming issues for Windclaw Raptors on Dantooine.
  • Added bg_token loot group to several newly introduced creatures.
  • Fixed Junk Dealer values; payouts now correctly display 250 credits.
  • Prevented non-Jedi-eligible players from receiving FRS points from specific NPC defeats.

Addendum: Creature Handler PvP

  • Creature Handler pets are now classified as pure PvE and cannot participate in PvP combat.
  • Creature Handlers may still engage in PvP using personal weapons and abilities.

Bellum Gero Patch Notes - February 10, 2026

This publish introduced new world content, Creature Handler upgrades, Jedi progression support updates, and critical stability fixes.

New Content and Additions

  • Torgas the Enslaver now spawns on Tatooine in the southeastern quadrant.
  • Added Azure Striped Peko on Endor.
  • Added bg_token loot group to Lord Nyax, with Holocron of Destiny drop chance.
  • New Clothing and Armor attachments added for Creature Handler and Ranger.

Fixes and Stability

  • House pack-up no longer counts the house as an item and now behaves correctly.
  • Fixed Powerups not deleting after all uses were consumed.
  • Blue Shadow Virus Bunker clone behavior now correctly resets poison and disease on death.
  • Reverted junk item stacking to prevent duplication exploits.
