Upcoming Features

The Ranger's Path

A new Scout and Ranger questline is coming soon to Bellum Gero. The Ranger's Path sends players deep into the wild frontiers to track deadly predators, investigate missing scouts, and defend settlers living beyond the safety of the major cities.

Each stage is built around survival and discovery, with dangerous creature ambushes, wilderness objectives, and an escalating hunt that leads to a legendary beast. Rangers who complete the full path will earn respect across the galaxy while proving they can master both the hunt and the terrain.

Final completion reward is guaranteed: 25,000 credits and a Creature Survey Tool (object/tangible/survey_tool/survey_tool_creature.iff). More details will be revealed in upcoming patch notes.

Patch Notes

Bellum Gero Patch Notes - 03/10/2026

This patch includes account-wide structure lot sharing, Padawan Trial reductions, player city fixes, new Ewok event updates, loot and DNA adjustments, Creature Survey tool support, rare crafted bonuses, faster pet maturation, and new monstrous insect hunting content across multiple planets.

Major Systems

  • Added account-wide structure lot sharing across all characters on the same account, with a 100-lot account cap.
  • Updated structure placement, transfer, guild hall transfer, and structure lookup systems to use account-wide lot tracking.
  • Removed the starter Helper Droid auto-spawn based on player feedback.

Jedi Progression

  • Reduced Padawan Trial PvE requirements from 5,000 points per phase to 1,750 points per phase.
  • Fixed Padawan Trial group credit so players must be within 32 meters of a kill to receive progress.

City and World Fixes

  • Fixed shuttle registration/loading issues for player cities.

New Content - The Lost Holocron

  • Added a new multi-stage questline, The Lost Holocron.
  • Quest begins with the Holocron Cartographer in Bestine, Tatooine.
  • Players must travel to hidden Force anomaly locations across multiple planets.
  • Each anomaly contains a Survey Stone that can be activated to begin the encounter.
  • Activating a stone spawns 3-5 hostile guardians near the player.
  • Defeating the guardians advances the quest and rewards a Holocron Fragment.
  • Quest progression is fully persistent across restarts and crashes.
  • Added a 20-hour completion cooldown per character.
  • Added a 10-minute anti-respawn lockout if guardians are spawned but not defeated.
  • Final reward for completing the full questline is a Holocron of Destiny.

Event Updates

  • Updated the scheduled event for March 15, 2026 to feature Giant Ewok tribe leaders attacking Ewokanda.
  • Added new Ewok event bosses: Graaku, Torga, and Nakku.
  • Updated Giant Ewok Warchief loot to include power crystals instead of weapon drops.

Loot and Boss Updates

  • Adjusted faction NPC loot group values to better match intended item rarity.
  • Reduced Radar Topography Screen drop weighting in faction loot tables.
  • Added custom names to Technical Console schematic loot items.
  • Added Nabahe and TraF to the named DJM boss pool.

Creature Survey System

  • Added a new Organic Survey Device / Creature Survey Tool for upcoming rewards.
  • Enabled Creature Survey tools to properly detect creature resources.
  • Set Creature Survey tool usage to rely on creature_knowledge, including range calculation and eligibility.

DNA and Bio-Engineer

  • Updated DNA sampling so level 300+ creatures always drop VHQ DNA samples.
  • Lower-level DNA sample quality now remains tied to the player's DNA harvesting skill.

Crafting

  • Added a small rare chance for crafted clothing, jewelry, and armor to roll bonus stat mods.
  • Prevented those random crafted bonus mods from carrying over into factory-produced items.

Creature Handler

  • Reduced pet maturation time so pets now fully mature in 3 days.

New Creature Content: Monstrous Insects

  • New monstrous insect creatures have been added across four planets as a source of Insect Meat and Scaley Hide for crafting.
  • All creature types are available for open-world hunting.
  • All appear as targets on Destroy Mission terminals.
  • All monstrous insects are aggressive and drop Insect Meat.
  • Beetle variants also drop Scaley Hide.

Lok (CL 26-35)

Creatures: Monstrous Lok Mite, Monstrous Kubaza Beetle, Monstrous Giant Kubaza (Boss)

Mission Targets: Monstrous Lok Mite Nest, Monstrous Kubaza Colony

Dantooine (CL 36-45)

Creatures: Monstrous Dantooine Mite, Monstrous Dantooine Beetle, Monstrous Giant Dantooine Mite (Boss)

Mission Targets: Monstrous Dantooine Mite Nest, Monstrous Dantooine Beetle Mound

Tatooine (CL 42-50)

Creatures: Monstrous Mound Mite, Monstrous Death Kreetle, Monstrous Rockmite, Monstrous Sand Beetle, Monstrous Overkreetle (Boss)

Mission Targets: Monstrous Kreetle Nest, Monstrous Rock Beetle Mound, Monstrous Sand Beetle Colony

Yavin IV (CL 46-50)

Creatures: Monstrous Tanc Mite Warrior, Monstrous Lurking Angler, Monstrous Mad Angler, Monstrous Giant Tanc Mite (Boss)

Mission Targets: Monstrous Tanc Mite Colony, Monstrous Angler Nest, Monstrous Mad Angler Den

Bellum Gero Patch Notes - March 3, 2026

This update introduces faction troop recruitment for player cities, adds a new artisan contract vendor in Coronet, and delivers DNA and quality-of-life balance adjustments.

New Feature: Player Cities - Recruit Faction Troops

Mayor Tool Added: Recruit Faction Troops

Available to Rebel and Imperial aligned Player Cities.

  • Mayors can recruit city defense troops using the City Treasury.
  • Each faction now has three troop tiers with increasing strength and increasing cost.
  • Troops are now fully integrated into the city system.
  • Configurable maximum troop cap via FactionTroopsMax.
  • Automatic cleanup occurs when troops are destroyed.
  • Persistence across server restarts now restores menu behavior, observer state, and home location data.
  • Mayors can remove placed troops via radial menu, and funds are returned to the City Treasury.

This system allows cities to invest in scalable NPC defenses while maintaining economic balance.

New Feature: Artisan Procurement Vendor (Coronet)

A new Artisan Procurement Vendor has arrived in Coronet, Corellia.

  • Offers rotating artisan crafting contracts.
  • Complete contracts for Credits.
  • Visit the vendor directly to view current available contracts.

A new way for crafters to earn steady income and stimulate the economy.

DNA System Adjustments

  • Reduced drop rate of looted DNA samples to preserve rarity and value.
  • DNA quality and type from harvesting are now properly determined by the DNA Harvesting Skill.

This strengthens Bio-Engineer progression and ties results directly to profession investment.

Quality of Life Improvements

  • Increased chance to successfully catch fish.
  • Reduced milking time so creatures can now be milked faster.
  • SEA Removal Tools can now be combined up to 50 charges via radial option.

As always, thank you for supporting Bellum Gero and helping shape the evolution of the galaxy. May the Force guide your path.
