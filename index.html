Mandalorian Way of Life

The Hunt Begins.

A galaxy-wide Bounty Hunter progression path is coming to Bellum Gero, built around honor, beskar, and the hard proof that only combat can provide. Each contract pushes you deeper into the creed, where reputation is earned world by world and every victory carries weight.

"Mando'ade daras drikar..."

01 — WHAT IS THIS

The Mandalorian Way of Life is a parallel progression system built for Bounty Hunters who want a deeper path beyond the standard profession climb. There are no guilds, no shortcuts, and no borrowed prestige. Instead, hunters move through a 10-world hunt system, completing each chapter to prove they belong on the road toward Mandalorian status.

Parallel Progression

Advance beside your profession path with a dedicated identity-driven journey built around the hunt.

Earned Status

Every chapter is personal. Progress comes from completed hunts and discipline, not social shortcuts.

02 — HOW YOU WILL BEGIN

There is no registration required. Travel to Tatooine, enter the Mos Eisley cantina district, and seek out the Mandalorian Recruiter. From the moment you engage, the system begins tracking your progress automatically and the first leg of the hunt comes online.

No Ceremony Needed

Show up ready to work. The path opens through action, not paperwork.

Automatic Tracking

Once you begin, your hunt progression follows you across the galaxy without extra setup.
